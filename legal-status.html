Legal & Regulatory Status

Section 8 Company Framework

AIEDR operates as a Section 8 company under the Companies Act, 2013. Section 8 entities are established for charitable purposes, including education, research, and public welfare, and operate on a not-for-profit basis.

  • No distribution of profits or dividends to members
  • Income applied solely toward institutional objectives
  • Enhanced governance and statutory oversight

This structure ensures transparency, accountability, and alignment with public interest mandates.
