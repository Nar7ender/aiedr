Founder & Academic Leadership

Institutional leadership rooted in five decades of academic governance, research, and higher-education administration.

Prof. H. L. Verma

Prof. H. L. Verma is a distinguished academic administrator and educationist with over five decades of experience in higher education, research, institutional governance, and academic leadership.

He is currently serving as Vice-Chancellor of Baba Mastnath University and has previously held senior leadership positions including Vice-Chancellor, President (Vice-Chancellor), Pro Vice-Chancellor, Professor, Dean of Management, and Head of Department across multiple universities in India.

His academic career spans teaching, research supervision, university administration, and policy-oriented leadership, with a strong focus on commerce, management education, entrepreneurship, and institutional development.

Prof. Verma has authored several academic books and published more than sixty peer-reviewed research papers in national and international journals.

Under his guidance, the Anandmayee Institute for Entrepreneurship Development & Research (AIEDR) has been established as a Section 8, not-for-profit institution dedicated to education, entrepreneurship development, applied research, and societal capacity building.
