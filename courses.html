Courses & Academic Programmes

Structured academic and professional programmes designed to strengthen knowledge, skills, and institutional capacity across finance, entrepreneurship, and applied technical domains.

Academic Approach

The courses offered by Anandmayee Institute for Entrepreneurship Development & Research (AIEDR) are designed as structured learning programmes combining conceptual foundations, applied frameworks, and real-world orientation.

AIEDR does not award degrees. Its programmes are offered as certificate, executive education, and capacity-building modules aligned with academic standards, industry relevance, and public-purpose objectives.

Programme in Accounting

This programme provides a structured understanding of accounting principles, financial statements, and regulatory frameworks relevant to business decision-making, entrepreneurship, and institutional governance.

Programme Objectives

  • Develop conceptual clarity in financial accounting and reporting
  • Understand the structure and interpretation of financial statements
  • Introduce auditing principles and internal control frameworks
  • Build familiarity with accounting standards and regulatory norms

Indicative Curriculum

  • Introduction to Accounting Concepts & Principles
  • Financial Statements: Balance Sheet, Income Statement, Cash Flows
  • Accounting Standards and Regulatory Environment
  • Cost Accounting & Managerial Decision-Making
  • Basics of Auditing and Internal Controls
  • Financial Analysis for Entrepreneurs and Managers

Target Participants

  • Entrepreneurs and business owners
  • Students of management, commerce, and economics
  • Working professionals seeking accounting literacy

Programme in Capital Markets

This programme offers a comprehensive introduction to capital markets, focusing on market structure, financial instruments, valuation, and investment decision-making.

Programme Objectives

  • Understand the functioning of equity and debt markets
  • Develop foundational valuation and investment analysis skills
  • Introduce derivatives, fixed income, and risk concepts
  • Enhance market literacy and analytical capability

Indicative Curriculum

  • Overview of Capital Markets and Market Participants
  • Equity Markets: Shares, Indices, and Trading Mechanisms
  • Debt Markets and Fixed Income Instruments
  • Fundamental and Basic Valuation Concepts
  • Introduction to Derivatives and Risk Management
  • Market Regulation and Investor Protection

Target Participants

  • Students and early-career professionals
  • Entrepreneurs and business decision-makers
  • Individuals seeking structured market understanding

Programme in HVAC Systems

This programme introduces participants to Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning (HVAC) systems with an emphasis on applied understanding, infrastructure operations, and energy efficiency.

Programme Objectives

  • Build conceptual understanding of HVAC system components
  • Understand energy efficiency and sustainability considerations
  • Introduce operational and maintenance perspectives
  • Develop interdisciplinary awareness for infrastructure projects

Indicative Curriculum

  • Introduction to HVAC Systems and Applications
  • Heating and Cooling Technologies
  • Ventilation, Indoor Air Quality, and Comfort Standards
  • Energy Efficiency and Sustainable Design
  • Basic System Design and Operational Considerations
  • Overview of Codes, Standards, and Best Practices

Target Participants

  • Engineering and infrastructure professionals
  • Facility managers and project managers
  • Entrepreneurs and decision-makers in built-environment sectors
