Our Philosophy & Memorandum of Association

Institutional Philosophy

AIEDR is founded on the belief that entrepreneurship, financial literacy, and institutional capability are foundational pillars for sustainable economic development.

The Institute adopts a long-term, systems-oriented approach to education, focusing not only on skill transmission but also on judgment, governance, and ethical responsibility.

Objects of the Memorandum of Association

  • To promote education, training, and research in entrepreneurship and enterprise development.
  • To conduct academic and applied research in accounting, finance, capital markets, and infrastructure systems.
  • To design and deliver structured academic programs, workshops, and certification courses.
  • To collaborate with educational institutions, industry bodies, and public agencies.
  • To operate strictly on a not-for-profit basis in furtherance of public educational objectives.
