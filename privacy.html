Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Anandmayee Institute for Entrepreneurship Development & Research (AIEDR) collects, uses, and protects information.

1. Introduction

Anandmayee Institute for Entrepreneurship Development & Research (AIEDR) is committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of individuals who access its website or communicate with the Institute. This Privacy Policy outlines the manner in which information is collected, used, stored, and safeguarded.

AIEDR is a Section 8 not-for-profit academic institution incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013. The Institute operates for educational, research, and public-purpose objectives and does not engage in commercial data exploitation.

2. Information Collected

AIEDR collects only minimal information necessary for institutional communication and academic purposes. The information collected may include:

  • Name and contact details voluntarily provided via email or forms
  • Institutional or professional affiliation (if shared)
  • General correspondence content
  • Basic technical information such as IP address and browser type (collected automatically by web servers)

AIEDR does not knowingly collect sensitive personal data unless explicitly required and voluntarily provided for a lawful purpose.

3. Use of Information

Information collected by AIEDR is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • Responding to academic, research, or institutional enquiries
  • Providing information about programmes, activities, or initiatives
  • Internal record keeping and institutional administration
  • Compliance with applicable legal or regulatory requirements

AIEDR does not sell, rent, trade, or commercially exploit personal information under any circumstances.

4. Disclosure of Information

Personal information is not disclosed to third parties except in the following limited circumstances:

  • When required by law, regulation, or court order
  • When necessary for compliance with statutory obligations
  • With explicit consent of the individual concerned

5. Data Security

AIEDR takes reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or disclosure. Access to information is restricted to authorized personnel only.

While reasonable safeguards are implemented, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure. Users are advised to exercise discretion when sharing information electronically.

6. Cookies & Website Analytics

The AIEDR website may use basic cookies or analytics tools to understand website usage patterns and improve user experience. These tools do not collect personally identifiable information.

Users may choose to disable cookies through their browser settings.

7. External Links

The AIEDR website may contain links to external websites for reference or informational purposes. AIEDR is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of external websites.

8. Children’s Privacy

AIEDR does not knowingly collect personal information from children below the age of 18. If such information is inadvertently received, it will be deleted upon identification.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

AIEDR reserves the right to update or revise this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in legal requirements or institutional practices. Any changes will be posted on this page.
