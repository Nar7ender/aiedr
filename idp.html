Institutional Development Plan (IDP)

A structured, long-term roadmap for academic excellence, research capacity, institutional governance, and societal impact.

Purpose of the Institutional Development Plan

The Institutional Development Plan (IDP) of Anandmayee Institute for Entrepreneurship Development & Research (AIEDR) outlines the strategic vision, priorities, and phased development of the Institute over the medium to long term. The IDP serves as a guiding document to ensure academic rigor, governance integrity, financial sustainability, and alignment with national education and skill development objectives.

As a Section 8 not-for-profit academic institution, AIEDR adopts a mission-driven, non-commercial approach to institutional growth, ensuring that all development initiatives directly contribute to education, research, and public benefit.

Institutional Vision

To emerge as a credible academic and research institution dedicated to entrepreneurship development, financial literacy, applied research, and institutional capacity building, contributing meaningfully to economic development and knowledge creation.

Institutional Mission

  • To deliver high-quality academic and professional programmes
  • To promote applied and policy-oriented research
  • To foster entrepreneurship and employability
  • To strengthen institutional governance and academic leadership
  • To contribute to national and regional development priorities

Strategic Development Pillars

1. Academic Programme Development

AIEDR will progressively expand its academic offerings across entrepreneurship, accounting, capital markets, management, and applied technical domains. Programmes will be designed to balance theoretical foundations with practical relevance and industry orientation.

  • Certificate, diploma, and executive education programmes
  • Modular and stackable learning structures
  • Continuous curriculum review and upgradation

2. Research & Knowledge Creation

Research forms a core component of AIEDR’s institutional mandate. The Institute will encourage faculty-led and collaborative research in entrepreneurship, finance, governance, and applied economics.

  • Working papers, case studies, and policy briefs
  • Industry and practitioner-oriented research
  • Academic conferences and research seminars

3. Faculty & Academic Leadership Development

AIEDR recognizes faculty quality and academic leadership as foundational to institutional credibility. The IDP emphasizes continuous faculty development and academic mentoring.

  • Faculty development programmes (FDPs)
  • Visiting faculty and academic advisors
  • Mentorship by senior academicians

4. Institutional Governance & Compliance

Robust governance structures will be established to ensure transparency, accountability, and compliance with statutory and regulatory requirements applicable to Section 8 institutions.

  • Board oversight and academic committees
  • Internal policies and standard operating procedures
  • Periodic audits and disclosures

5. Infrastructure & Digital Enablement

AIEDR will adopt a scalable, technology-enabled institutional model, leveraging digital platforms for teaching, learning, and research dissemination.

  • Learning management systems (LMS)
  • Digital libraries and repositories
  • Hybrid and online programme delivery

Phased Development Roadmap

Phase I: Foundation (Years 1–2)

  • Institutional setup and governance framework
  • Launch of initial academic programmes
  • Faculty onboarding and academic processes
  • Compliance and statutory registrations

Phase II: Consolidation (Years 3–5)

  • Expansion of programme portfolio
  • Strengthening of research output
  • Industry and institutional collaborations
  • Formalization of quality assurance mechanisms

Phase III: Maturity (Beyond 5 Years)

  • Establishment of specialized research centers
  • National and international academic partnerships
  • Policy engagement and advisory initiatives
  • Long-term institutional sustainability

Expected Outcomes & Impact

Through systematic implementation of the IDP, AIEDR aims to contribute to:

  • Enhanced access to quality education and skill development
  • Improved entrepreneurship and employability outcomes
  • Knowledge creation through applied research
  • Strengthened institutional governance and leadership
