Advancing Entrepreneurship, Research & Institutional Capacity

Anandmayee Institute for Entrepreneurship Development & Research (AIEDR) is a Section 8, not-for-profit institution dedicated to education, entrepreneurship development, applied research, and societal advancement.

Institutional Mandate

AIEDR operates as a public-purpose institution committed to strengthening entrepreneurship ecosystems, academic research, employability, and sustainable development through structured programmes, research initiatives, and institutional collaborations.

Entrepreneurship Development

Promoting entrepreneurial thinking, enterprise creation, and innovation through structured training programmes and capacity-building initiatives.

Education & Skill Development

Delivering academic and professional learning programmes aligned with higher education standards and industry requirements.

Research & Policy Engagement

Conducting applied and policy-oriented research in management, entrepreneurship, and socio-economic development.

Core Pillars

  • Entrepreneurship education and enterprise development
  • Academic research and knowledge dissemination
  • Skill development and employability enhancement
  • Institutional capacity building and collaboration
  • Community development and social impact
