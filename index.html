Institution Overview

Anandmayee Institute for Entrepreneurship Development & Research (AIEDR) is a Section 8, not-for-profit institution established with the objective of advancing education, entrepreneurship, research, and skill development in alignment with national priorities and the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020.

The Institute operates as a public-purpose organisation and does not award degrees. Its activities are focused on capacity building, academic programmes, research support, and institutional collaboration.
