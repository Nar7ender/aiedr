Who We Are

The Anandmayee Institute for Entrepreneurship Development & Research (AIEDR) has been established as an independent academic and research institution with the objective of fostering entrepreneurial capability, financial understanding, and applied institutional research.

AIEDR operates at the intersection of education, research, and policy-oriented capacity building. Our programs are designed to bridge the gap between theoretical instruction and real-world application, particularly in areas such as accounting, capital markets, infrastructure systems, and enterprise development.

Academic Rigor

Structured curricula grounded in accounting standards, market theory, governance frameworks, and applied systems thinking.

Entrepreneurial Focus

Emphasis on enterprise creation, financial decision-making, and operational sustainability.

Public Purpose

Operated under a not-for-profit mandate to advance education and research for public benefit.
